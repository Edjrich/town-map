About

I'm Eric Richardson and I am a front-end developer. I'm based out of east-end Toronto, and recent graduate from Juno College of Technology's Web Development Bootcamp. I write clean, responsive, and accessible code with a keen eye for detail. I'm a creative problem solver with a unique ability to connect with others. I am a lifelong learner, and always challenging myself with something new.

Before coming to webdev, I was a guitar teacher for over a decade. I love to teach, and teaching has given me the ability to confidently and clearly communicate complex ideas. My background in music has taught me to fail my way to success. Whether it's learning a new song or a new language, mistakes are an important part of the learning process.

When I'm not sharpening my front-end skills, I sometimes do other things! These can include (and are mostly limited to) making music, playing video games or board games, cooking, taking my dog out for walks, and watching unhealthy amounts of baseball.
